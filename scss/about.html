Science & Tech

Our school's technology program provides students with access to resources that enhance learning, prepare them for the digital age, and develop their problem-solving and critical thinking skills.

The school is also equiped with a modern Lab facilty for Both Chemestry,Physics and Biology Practicals

Music and Drama Club

Our school's music and drama programs provide students with the opportunity to explore their passion for the arts, express themselves creatively, and develop skills via a variety of classes and performances.

Games and Sports

Our school's games and sports program offers students of all skill levels the opportunity to express their creativity and develop valuable skills through a wide range of activities involving footbal for both boys and girls,Athletics,volleyball,Rugby and Netball.

Healthy food

Our school's offers healthy and delicious food options for students, providing them with the energy and nutrition they need to excel in their studies and other activities.

Educational Tour

Our school's educational tour program offers students the opportunity to learn outside the classroom and gain hands-on experience through visits to museums, historical sites, and other educational destinations for those Students who have intrest in Geography.
